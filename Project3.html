LLM-Enhanced Time Series Forecasting with
Federal Reserve SR 11-7 Model Risk Governance

This project explores the intersection of large language models and regulatory compliance by building an SR 11-7 compliant forecasting framework. Using the FNSPID dataset—29.7 million stock prices and 15.7 million financial news records spanning 24 years—we benchmark traditional time series models, deep learning architectures, and LLM-enhanced approaches while documenting the full model governance lifecycle required for production deployment in banking.
